National Park GeoGuesser

Based upon the popular game geoguesser, this project aimed to create a CNN that could predict a location given a landscape image. Similiar to the real game, images were pulled from Google StreetView using their API. To stay within the non-commercial use only 8,000 images were pulled from 8 different national parks. Various networks were then trained on this data. After training two networks were able to predict locations with over 75% accuracy.
